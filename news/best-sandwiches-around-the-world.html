尋味全球：盤點世界最棒的 10 款經典三明治
Savoring the Globe: 10 of the World’s Best and Most Iconic Sandwiches ​

世界上是否有任何一種食物能像三明治一樣，既謙遜低調又廣受全球大眾熱愛？雖然每個人心中的夢幻款式可能不盡相同——或許是香脆的日式炸豬排三明治，又或是料多實在的南美切維托三明治（Chivito）——但地球上幾乎沒有任何一個國家，不曾依靠「麵包夾入萬物」的經典組合來滿足味蕾渴望。探尋全球各地的三明治，在某種程度上，就如同環遊世界一般精彩。

Is there a food more humble yet universally adored than the sandwich? And while one person’s go-to hamburger snack might be another’s katsu sando or chivito, there’s hardly a country on the planet that doesn’t turn to some type of bread with something stuffed inside it to cure a craving. Traveling the world’s sandwiches is, in a way, like traveling the world.

為了幫助您鎖定下一次旅行的完美美食地圖，我們特別精選了 CNN 評選全球最棒的 10 款最具文化代表性與驚艷味蕾的傳奇三明治，帶您展開一場極致的舌尖環球之旅！

To help narrow your choices for the sandwich to plan your next culinary trip around, we have curated 10 of the world's best, most culturally iconic and mouth-watering sandwiches from CNN's list for a spectacular global tour!

🥪 傳奇三明治名人堂
The Hall of Legendary Global Sandwiches ​

1. 越式法國麵包 — 越南 (Bánh Mì, Vietnam)

Asia

作為法國殖民時期的烹飪遺存，越南人民發揮極致創意，將原本單調的法式長棍麵包融入在地風味，重新詮釋成獨一無二的「越式法國麵包（Bánh mì）」。如今，越式麵包的攤車遍佈胡志明市及全越南的街角巷弄，並深深俘獲了全球食客的心。經典版本以豬肉為骨幹，夾入鮮美彈牙的越式扎肉（chả lụa）、清爽酸甜的醃蘿蔔絲、香菜、抹上滑順香濃的美乃滋與香料。每一口都充滿了酥脆、清新、鹹香與多層次，堪稱美味絕倫！

A culinary remnant of French colonialism, the baguette sandwich was reinterpreted to their own taste by the Vietnamese. Bánh mì are now sold from food carts on nearly every street corner in Ho Chi Minh City and across Vietnam, and are widely loved well beyond the country’s borders. The classic version is pork-based, starring chả lụa (Vietnamese-style pork roll), shredded pickled carrots, pickled daikon, cilantro, mayonnaise and spices. The taste is crunchy, fresh, savory and utterly delicious.

2. 庫克先生/夫人脆烤三明治 — 法國 (Croque Monsieur/Madame, France)

Europe

這是來自法國咖啡館文化的極致輕食代表。Croque（意為「香脆」）這款完美的香烤奇蹟分為兩種經典化身：庫克先生（Croque Monsieur）是將法式白麵包抹上厚厚的黃油與法式白醬（Béchamel），夾入香嫩的火腿薄片與滿滿的瑞士艾曼塔（Emmental）或葛瑞爾（Gruyère）起司，煎至外酥內軟、雙重爆漿；而庫克夫人（Croque Madame）則更上一層樓——在微焦的起司頂端放上一顆飽滿完美的太陽煎蛋，將刀尖輕輕劃開，濃郁的蛋黃液緩緩流下，幸福感瞬間爆棚。

An archetypal sandwich from France that originated as "un snack" in French cafes, this crunchy (croquant) marvel comes in male and female incarnations. For the croque monsieur, slices of white bread are stuffed with thinly sliced ham and emmental or gruyere, buttered, coated in rich béchamel, and pan-fried or baked to golden perfection. For the croque madame, the egg component is served sunny-side-up atop the bubbling cheese sandwich, letting the rich yolk mingle with the molten cheese.

3. 極厚炸豬排三明治 — 日本 (Katsu Sando, Japan)

Asia

厚實飽滿、經過反覆拍打揉捏的極嫩豬排，裹上鬆軟的日式麵包粉（Panko）炸至外皮金黃酥脆、內裡肉汁橫溢。接著將其浸入鹹甜濃郁的日式豬排醬汁，夾入蓬鬆濕潤、帶有溫和奶香的日式生吐司（Shokupan）中，並點綴爽脆的細高麗菜絲。這款極厚炸豬排三明治不僅是日本便利商店與高級餐廳的超人氣招牌，更是將西方洋食（Yōshoku）與東方匠人精神融合的極致美味結晶。

A deep-fried pork cutlet — pounded, breaded with airy panko, and fried until golden and incredibly juicy — is drenched in savory-sweet tonkatsu sauce and tucked into a fluffy Japanese white milk bread called shokupan. Considered a masterpiece of yōshoku cuisine (Western-influenced), the katsu sando is garnished with crunchy ribbons of cabbage, offering a glorious contrast of textures in every bite.

4. 伊比利火腿三明治 — 西班牙 (Bocadillo de Jamón Ibérico, Spain)

Europe

普通火腿三明治比比皆是，但唯獨這款能將「極簡主義」與「極致奢華」完美融合。這款西班牙極致美味的主角，是世界上最頂級的伊比利火腿（Jamón Ibérico）——來自橡子餵養的黑蹄豬，經過數十個月風乾，油脂入口即化、散發沉穩優雅的木質堅果香氣。廚師將現切的極薄火腿片堆疊在酥脆硬挺的西班牙長棍麵包上，淋上香濃的特級初榨橄欖油。有時會抹上新鮮番茄泥（Pan con Tomate），但火腿無庸置疑是這場味覺盛宴的唯一焦點。

There are ho-hum ham sandwiches, and then there’s this simple handheld masterpiece featuring some of the world’s finest jamón — luxurious, acorn-fed Iberian ham. The paper-thin sliced meat is piled onto crusty rustic bread that’s brushed or drizzled with extra virgin olive oil. Fresh grated tomatoes (Pan con Tomate) can be added, but the nutty, melt-in-your-mouth ham is undoubtedly the absolute star of the show.

5. 新英格蘭經典龍蝦堡 — 美國 (Lobster Roll, United States)

Americas

在炎熱夏日的陽光海岸，最極致的犒賞莫過於一只堆滿大塊鮮甜清蒸龍蝦肉的奢華龍蝦堡。在緬因州（Maine），鮮美彈牙的冷鮮龍蝦肉與滑順美乃滋、新鮮檸檬汁與香料輕輕拌勻，夾入微烤過、外側酥黃的熱狗堡中；而鄰近的康乃狄克州（Connecticut），人們則更偏愛熱食——將剛起鍋溫熱多汁的龍蝦肉淋上濃郁的融化熱奶油。不論是哪種流派，都是不容錯過的頂級海鮮饗宴！

A summertime coastal treat dear to New Englanders, the lobster roll is piled high with massive chunks of sweet, freshly steamed lobster meat. In Maine, the lobster is served chilled and mixed gently with mayonnaise, lemon juice, and seasonings, tucked inside a toasted split-top hot dog bun. Over in Connecticut, it’s served warm and drenched in molten drawn butter. Both styles offer a scenic and unforgettable taste of the sea.

6. 丹麥單面黑麥三明治 — 丹麥 (Smørrebrød, Denmark)

Europe

這款風靡整個北歐、更是丹麥國寶級美饌的「單面三明治」，其名字直譯僅是「塗抹黃油的麵包」，但其實際的精緻程度堪稱盤中藝術。它以紮實酸香、富含穀物香氣的丹麥黑麥麵包（Rugbrød）為底座，抹上一層香濃黃油，其上堆疊著無限豐富的食材組合：從醃漬鯡魚、鮮甜的北極小粉蝦，到軟嫩的半熟蛋、鮮切冷牛肉。在精緻的北歐美學堅持下，每款三明治的擺盤都如微縮花園般精美，令人賞心悅目、欲罷不能。

Beloved all over Scandinavia but particularly iconic as Denmark’s national culinary dish, this open-faced sandwich translates simply to “buttered bread” — but smørrebrød is so much more. Using dense, sour rye bread (rugbrød) as the canvas, toppings include hundreds of elaborate combinations ranging from pickled herring and tiny pink cold-water shrimp to sliced boiled eggs and rare roast beef. The sandwiches are as gorgeous to look at as they are delicious to eat.

7. 傳奇烤肉沙威瑪 — 中東 (Shawarma, Middle East)

Middle East

「沙威瑪（Shawarma）」的名字源自阿拉伯語中的「旋轉」一詞，指的就是在垂直烤肉架上緩緩旋轉、接受烈火淬煉的醃漬厚實肉塊。隨著肉塊外層烤至微微焦脆、肉汁橫溢，廚師用利刃片下香嫩的烤肉（通常為羊肉、牛肉或雞肉），塞入溫熱濕潤、帶有嚼勁的皮塔餅（Pita）或薄餅中，鋪上鮮切番茄、洋蔥、香菜，並淋上濃郁的芝麻醬（Tahini）與香辣醬汁。這款傳奇街頭美食歷經千年，並演化為希臘旋轉烤肉（Gyro）與德國土耳其烤肉（Döner Kebab），風靡全球。

Shawarma’s name comes from the Arabic word for “turning” — a reference to how this favorite Middle Eastern sandwich’s meaty filling cooks on a vertical rotisserie spit. While there are many variations of this popular street food, its base is spiced meats shaved from the spit and tucked into a light pocket of pita bread or flatbread, topped with fresh tomatoes, onions, parsley, and savory sesame tahini sauce.

8. 費城起司牛肉堡 — 美國 (Philly Cheesesteak, United States)

Americas

來自兄弟之城費城的靈魂美味，是一場簡單卻極具張力的熱熔重工業美學饗宴。香氣沖天的鐵板上，切成薄片的肋眼牛肉（Ribeye）與鮮甜的洋蔥、青椒丁一同快速翻炒。在起鍋前，覆蓋上厚厚的普羅沃洛內起司（Provolone）或傳奇的 Cheez Whiz 起司醬，待其完全融化並包裹住每一絲牛肉纖維後，剷入長條狀的義式霍奇麵包（Hoagie Bun）中。麵包吸收了濃郁的肉汁與熱起司，每一口都是罪惡感與極致美味的終極碰撞！

Mouthwateringly simple, the City of Brotherly Love’s most beloved sandwich is a delectable hot mess layered with ribeye steak sliced razor-thin on a flat-top grill, oozing sheets of provolone cheese or Cheez Whiz, and sauteed peppers and caramelized onions. Enveloped inside a soft, chewy hoagie bun, it represents the ultimate comfort food experience.

9. 古巴熱壓三明治 — 古巴/美國 (Cuban Sandwich, Cuba/United States)

Americas

這款發源於古巴煙草工人、並在美國佛羅里達州（邁阿密與坦帕）發揚光大的傳奇三明治，是熱壓三明治界中無法被超越的皇冠。兩片蓬鬆柔軟的古巴麵包內，層層鋪上特製柑橘香料慢烤豬肉（Lechón）、煙燻火腿、香濃瑞士起司、酸黃瓜片，並抹上醇厚的黃芥末。最後放入雙面鐵板壓烤機中，以高溫重壓至麵包外皮呈金黃薄脆、內裡瑞士起司完全融化，將所有材料鎖在一起。外脆內嫩、微酸解膩，簡直堪稱完美！

Originally a staple in Cuba, this sandwich has become a massive favorite in Florida. Layered with slow-roasted citrus-marinated pork, smoked ham, tangy pickles, yellow mustard, and rich Swiss cheese, it is pressed between buttered slices of fluffy Cuban bread inside a plancha press until the exterior is shatteringly crisp and the cheese is beautifully melted. Hearty, zesty, and utterly satisfying.

10. 皇家小黃瓜下午茶三明治 — 英國 (Cucumber Sandwich, United Kingdom)

Europe

在口味厚重、肉食堆疊的三明治光譜另一端，這款極致優雅的小黃瓜三明治則是英國傳統貴族下午茶的靈魂代表。它將極其柔軟的白麵包完全切除麵包皮，抹上一層綿密細緻的優質黃油，鋪上切成薄如蟬翼、經過輕輕鹽漬脫水的新鮮英國小黃瓜片，撒上少許新鮮蒔蘿（Dill）或薄荷，切成精緻對稱的三角形。搭配一壺剛泡好的大吉嶺紅茶，品味淡淡小麥清香與黃瓜的冰脆甘甜，彷彿置身於維多利亞時代的優雅庭園中。

On the dainty side of the sandwich spectrum, cucumber sandwiches are a traditional English afternoon tea staple, representing ultimate elegance. Extra-soft white bread with the crusts carefully removed gets layered with razor-thin English cucumbers (lightly salted and drained), butter, fresh dill, and a dash of white pepper. Cut into neat triangles, they pair beautifully with a hot pot of Earl Grey or Darjeeling tea.
