🥃 明星龍舌蘭酒大評測：誰是真正的液體黃金？
The Best Celebrity Tequilas, Tasted and Ranked by Professionals ​

龍舌蘭酒（Tequila）不再只是派對上的射擊酒（Shots），它已經成為全球最受矚目的烈酒之一。而在這股熱潮中，好萊塢明星與體育巨星的參與無疑起到了推波助瀾的作用。從喬治·克隆尼（George Clooney）的 Casamigos 獲得巨大成功開始，無數名人紛紛創立或投資自己的龍舌蘭品牌。但這些名人品牌究竟是真材實料，還是僅僅依靠明星光環包裝？

Tequila is no longer just a party shot; it has become one of the most highly regarded spirits globally. Driving this boom is a wave of Hollywood stars and sports icons launching their own brands. Ever since the massive success of George Clooney’s Casamigos, numerous celebrities have jumped on the bandwagon. But are these celebrity bottles actually premium spirits, or just high-priced star power in fancy packaging?

為了找出答案，CNN Underscored 團隊攜手紐約知名酒吧 Bar Bastion 的首席調酒師安娜·莫拉伊斯（Ana Moraes）進行了一場深度盲測。莫拉伊斯強調，品鑑優質龍舌蘭酒的關鍵在於感受純淨的藍色韋伯龍舌蘭（Blue Weber agave）風味、口感的滑順度，以及最重要的——是否含有不必要的人工添加物（Additives）。

To find the truth, the CNN Underscored team collaborated with Ana Moraes, head bartender at New York’s acclaimed Bar Bastion, for an in-depth blind tasting. Moraes emphasized that the key to evaluating premium tequila lies in identifying the pure flavor of Blue Weber agave, the spirit's smoothness, and most importantly, the presence of unnecessary additives.

以下是我們為您精選的本次評測中的獲獎得主，以及其他幾款備受矚目的名人龍舌蘭酒。無論你是想要純飲、調配雞尾酒，還是尋找一款適合派對的酒，這裡都有最權威的推薦。

Below are the top winners selected from the rigorous tasting sessions, along with several other highly discussed celebrity tequila brands. Whether you prefer to sip it neat, shake up a cocktail, or search for the perfect party shot, here is your definitive guide.

🏆 頂級明星龍舌蘭酒得獎名單
The Award Winners of Celebrity Tequilas ​

🥇 Flecha Azul Blanco Tequila

馬克·華伯格 / Best Overall

獲獎榮譽：最優秀明星龍舌蘭酒（總冠軍） | 參考價格：約 $45 - $50 美元
由好萊塢巨星馬克·華伯格（Mark Wahlberg）投資並代言。這款酒 100% 無添加劑（additive-free），口感極其純淨絲滑。它散發著濃郁的熟龍舌蘭香氣，並伴隨著明亮的柑橘與胡椒微甜。調酒師莫拉伊斯盛讚它「非常乾淨、乾爽，口感豐富，幾乎可以感受到那種絲滑感在舌尖綻放。」無論是純飲還是調製經典瑪格麗特都是完美首選。

Award: Best Celebrity Tequila Overall | Price: ~$45 - $50
Backed and co-owned by Hollywood A-lister Mark Wahlberg. Completely additive-free, this tequila is incredibly clean and silky on the palate. It features rich cooked agave aromas paired with bright citrus and a touch of sweet pepper notes. Moraes praised it as "very clean, dry, and complex, with a wonderful silky mouthfeel." It shines exceptionally whether sipped neat or shaken into a perfect margarita.

🥈 Teremana Tequila Blanco

巨石強森 / Best for Citrus Lovers

獲獎榮譽：最佳柑橘風味（亞軍） | 參考價格：約 $30 美元
由「巨石」強森（Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson）創立，是近年來全球銷量增長最快的烈酒品牌之一。它的風味極具表現力，帶有濃郁的青檸、葡萄柚與檸檬皮的明亮柑橘香氣，口感十分清新。調酒師認為它是調製 Paloma（帕洛瑪）或各類柑橘系雞尾酒的絕佳基酒，性價比極高。

Award: Runner-up & Best for Citrus Lovers | Price: ~$30
Founded by Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson, Teremana is one of the fastest-growing tequila brands in history. It features an expressive profile with bright lime, grapefruit, and lemon zest notes, creating an incredibly refreshing finish. Moraes recommended it as a stellar, high-value option for Palomas or other citrus-forward cocktails.

🔥 Pantalones Organic Tequila Blanco

馬修·麥康納夫婦 / Best for Shots

獲獎榮譽：最適合一口乾（Shot） | 參考價格：約 $40 美元
由奧斯卡影帝馬修·麥康納（Matthew McConaughey）與其妻子卡蜜拉（Camila）聯手創立。這款有機認證的龍舌蘭酒風味強勁而狂野，帶有明顯的粗磨胡椒辛香與熱帶水果香氣，尾韻帶著些許薄荷的清涼。它那厚重、溫暖且充滿力量的喉韻，使其成為派對上最完美的 Shot 擔當，能瞬間點燃氣氛。

Award: Best for Shots | Price: ~$40
Founded by Oscar-winning actor Matthew McConaughey and his wife Camila. This certified organic tequila delivers a bold, punchy profile loaded with cracked pepper, tropical fruits, and a hint of cooling mint. Its warming, powerful finish makes it the absolute best choice for taking shots and starting a lively party.

💎 Cincoro Blanco Tequila

麥可·喬丹 / Best Splurge-worthy

獲獎榮譽：最值得奢華享受的精品 | 參考價格：約 $75 - $90 美元
由籃球之神麥可·喬丹（Michael Jordan）與多位 NBA 球隊老闆聯合創立，瓶身設計細長優雅、形似龍舌蘭葉，盡顯貴氣。這款酒帶有奢華的香草、焦糖與熟柑橘的微甜香氣。雖然價格不菲，但其絲絨般的圓潤口感與無與倫比的精緻外觀，非常適合佳節送禮或在特殊時刻品啜。

Award: Best Splurge-worthy | Price: ~$75 - $90
Co-founded by NBA legend Michael Jordan and fellow team owners, Cincoro comes in an elegant, towering bottle inspired by the agave leaf. It boasts rich, luxurious notes of sweet vanilla, warm caramel, and cooked citrus. While highly expensive, its velvety smoothness and stunning presentation make it perfect for gifting or savoring during special celebrations.

👀 備受矚目的熱門名人品牌
Other Highly Discussed Celebrity Brands ​

🍀 Casamigos Blanco Tequila

喬治·克隆尼 / Smoothest Favorite

品牌亮點：極致順口的大眾寵兒 | 參考價格：約 $45 美元
作為名人龍舌蘭酒的開山鼻祖與黃金標準，由喬治·克隆尼創立。它以極高比例的甜香草與奶油焦糖香氣著稱，口感絲滑無比，幾乎感受不到任何烈酒的灼燒感。雖然部分追求傳統的老饕質疑其使用了添加劑，但無庸置疑，它是最受大眾喜愛、門檻最低的「超滑順」明星代表。

Highlights: The Smoothest Fan Favorite | Price: ~$45
The pioneer and gold standard of celebrity tequilas, co-founded by George Clooney. It is universally famous for its sweet vanilla and rich butterscotch notes, with an ultra-smooth profile that has almost zero burn. While some tequila purists debate its sweetness, it remains the ultimate crowd-pleasing, easy-drinking celebrity classic.

🌴 818 Tequila Blanco

坎達兒·珍娜 / Sweetest Profile

品牌亮點：香甜濃郁的熱帶椰香 | 參考價格：約 $35 美元
由超級名模坎達兒·珍娜（Kendall Jenner）創立，品牌名稱源自加州卡拉巴薩斯（Calabasas）的郵遞區號。這款酒在盲測中被公認為「最香甜」的款式，滿溢著烤椰子、甜香草以及成熟香蕉與芒果等熱帶水果的濃郁香氣。對於初次接觸龍舌蘭、或偏愛香醇甜美風味的人來說，它是最完美的入門選擇。

Highlights: Sweetest & Most Tropical | Price: ~$35
Launched by supermodel Kendall Jenner and named after her Calabasas area code. 818 stands out as the sweetest tequila in the lineup, bursting with heavy notes of toasted coconut, rich vanilla, and ripe tropical fruits like banana and mango. It is a fantastic entry point for beginners who prefer a dessert-like, highly aromatic spirit.

🪵 Santo Tequila Añejo

Guy Fieri & Sammy Hagar / Aged Classic

品牌亮點：純淨無添加的橡木桶老酒 | 參考價格：約 $60 美元
由搖滾巨星薩米·哈加爾（Sammy Hagar）與「美國美食天王」蓋伊·費里（Guy Fieri）聯手打造。這款 Añejo（陳釀老酒）在橡木桶中慢熬陳釀 24 個月，100% 無人造添加劑。它完美地融合了熟龍舌蘭的草本清香，與橡木桶帶來的雪松、肉桂、煙燻胡椒與可可豆香氣，層次極其豐富，非常適合威士忌愛好者純飲。

Highlights: Best Additive-Free Aged | Price: ~$60
Crafted by rock legend Sammy Hagar and culinary icon Guy Fieri. This Añejo expression is aged for 24 months in oak barrels and is 100% additive-free. It offers a deeply layered profile combining baked agave herbs with oak wood, cedar, cinnamon, and smoky dark chocolate. It is an exceptional sipping spirit for whiskey enthusiasts.

💡 調酒師盲測結論：名人酒真的值得買嗎？
The Verdict: Is Celebrity Tequila Worth the Price? ​

首席調酒師莫拉伊斯與評測小組得出的結論是：確實值得，但需要挑選。 像 Flecha Azul 與 Santo 這樣堅持 100% 無添加（Additive-free）古法釀造的品牌，提供了頂級工藝龍舌蘭的豐富與深度；而 Teremana 和 Pantalones 則在日常調酒與派對上展現了極高的實用性與性價比。選購名人龍舌蘭不只是為明星光環買單，更是一場體驗多元、獨特烈酒風味的精彩旅程。

The verdict from Moraes and the tasting panel is: Yes, but pick wisely. Brands like Flecha Azul and Santo, which stick to 100% additive-free traditional methods, provide the complex depth of top-tier artisanal tequila. Meanwhile, Teremana and Pantalones offer incredible versatility and value for casual sipping and cocktails. Buying celebrity tequila isn't just about paying for star power; it's an exciting journey into the diverse, premium flavors of Mexico's finest spirit.
