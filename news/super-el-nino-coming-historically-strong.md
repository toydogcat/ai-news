<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">聖嬰現象（El Niño）正以遠超氣象科學家預期的驚人步伐在太平洋海域迅速崛起，各國氣象專家嚴正警告，在即將到來的今年秋冬季節，該現象演化為極為罕見、且極具歷史性破壞力的「超級聖嬰（Super El Niño）」的機率正呈現指數級激增。</p>
  <p class="en-text">El Niño is emerging even faster than expected in the Pacific Ocean and odds are increasing that it could become historically strong — a rare “Super” El Niño — by fall or winter.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">這是根據美國國家海洋暨大氣總署（NOAA）轄下的氣候預測中心最新發布的權威報告所指出的核心警訊。報告中明確強調：今年這波聖嬰現象在其發展的顛峰期中，強度能夠問鼎「強烈」或「極度強烈」等級的概率高達 **三分之二（2 in 3）**。</p>
  <p class="en-text">This is according to a just-released update from NOAA’s Climate Prediction Center that says there is a 2 in 3 chance that El Niño’s peak strength will be strong or very strong.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">聖嬰現象是一種大規模自然氣候循環震盪週期，當熱帶太平洋中東部海水表面水溫顯著升高到一定閥值時，就會引發大氣層上空信風帶的顛覆性轉變，進而對全球天氣秩序產生難以估量的多米諾骨牌效應。</p>
  <p class="en-text">El Niño is a natural climate cycle that happens when the tropical Pacific Ocean warms enough to trigger shifts in wind patterns throughout the atmosphere, which has a ripple effect on weather conditions worldwide.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">受此影響，部分脆弱地區可能會突發極端大範圍旱災與熱浪，大幅升高森林野火的危機並重創民生及灌溉用水的供應鏈；反觀其他地區則可能因源源不絕的水氣被吸走，面臨豪雨傾盆甚至淪為澤國的無情打擊。聖嬰現象無遠弗屆的連鎖干擾，甚至會在大洋彼岸重創大西洋的颶風季。而在全球宏觀尺度上，聖嬰更是將已因人為因素而極速上升的世界均溫，再度狠狠地往上推高到下一個恐怖的臨界高峰。聖嬰現象的力道越是猛烈，上述這些災難情節降臨人間的威脅也就隨之倍增。</p>
  <p class="en-text">Droughts and heat waves can flourish in some regions, fueling wildfire danger and water supply concerns, while others are swamped by flooding rainfall. El Niño’s far-reaching effects can also stymie the Atlantic hurricane season. On a larger scale, it causes already rising global temperatures from human-caused climate change to spike even higher. Stronger El Niños make all these impacts more likely.</p>
</div>

<h2 class="bilingual-header">
  <span class="zh-text">🌡️ 「攝氏2.0度」死線：解密超級聖嬰的科學定義</span>
  <span class="en-text">The 2°C Redline: Decoding the Science of Super El Niño</span>
</h2>

<div style="text-align: center; margin: 24px 0 24px 0;">
  <img src="https://media.cnn.com/api/v1/images/stellar/prod/01-web-sst-nina34-051326.png?c=original" alt="Pacific Ocean SST Nino 3.4 Region Map" style="border-radius: 12px; max-width: 100%; box-shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.1);" />
  <p style="font-size: 0.85rem; color: var(--vp-c-text-3); margin-top: 6px;">🗺️ 科學觀測：地圖中的紅色方框即為科學家嚴密監控太平洋海水表面溫度的 Nino 3.4 關鍵指標區域。</p>
</div>

<div class="dish-card">
  <div class="dish-card-header">
    <h3 class="dish-card-title">升溫界線的定性解讀</h3>
    <span class="dish-card-badge">科學指南 1</span>
  </div>
  <div class="bilingual-paragraph">
    <p class="zh-text">聖嬰現象大約每隔 2 至 7 年就會重新輪迴肆虐一次，完整週期往往跨度 9 至 12 個月。科學家主要是通過測量赤道太平洋特定海域（通常為 Niño 3.4 區）表層海水偏離歷史長期均溫的極限跨度，來為其「威力等級」打分。此氣候怪獸通常會在北半球即將步入寒冬之際，抵達其發展的最全盛期。</p>
    <p class="en-text">El Niño occurs about every two to seven years and lasts nine to 12 months. Its strength is measured by how far water temperatures rise above average in a patch of the equatorial Pacific Ocean, and it usually peaks in the Northern Hemisphere’s winter.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bilingual-paragraph">
    <p class="zh-text">只要監測區水溫在相當一段時間內持續偏高超過攝氏 0.5 度，氣象界即會宣布「弱聖嬰」發端。但若要攀上氣象科學的最高武裝警戒級別——即所谓的「極強聖嬰」或「超級聖嬰」，該區域海水表層溫度的偏差幅度就必須整整飆破 **攝氏 2.0 度** 以上的大關。</p>
    <p class="en-text">Weak El Niño conditions develop once the temperature rises over 0.5 degrees Celsius above average for an extended period of time. Water temperatures must be more than 2 degrees above average for it to be considered a very strong or Super El Niño.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="dish-card">
  <div class="dish-card-header">
    <h3 class="dish-card-title">暖水團蓄勢待發：九成機率坐實</h3>
    <span class="dish-card-badge">科學指南 2</span>
  </div>
  <div class="bilingual-paragraph">
    <p class="zh-text">氣候預測中心在最新的月度數據更新中透露：目前該監測區的平均海水表層溫度雖然尚落於攝氏 0.5 度的及格線之下，但目前的動力模型高度一致地指向最快在下個月內就將勢如破竹般破關而上。相較於上個月多數專家還普遍預測 6 月前都會是波瀾不驚的「中性」天氣，這次大氣科學的預報轉彎可以說是吹響了一場劇烈且迫切的氣溫升級號角。</p>
    <p class="en-text">The average water temperature is just below the 0.5-degree threshold right now, but it’s now expected to climb above it by next month, according to Thursday’s monthly update from the Climate Prediction Center. That’s a notable change from last month’s update, which favored neutral conditions — neither El Niño or its cooler counterpart La Niña — through June.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bilingual-paragraph">
    <p class="zh-text">氣象數據更無情預警，該聖嬰現象在越過盛夏與仲秋後只會日趨壯大。而今冬受其掌控的發生概率現已向上推升至無法忽視的 **96%**，幾乎可斷言其發展已成無法逆轉的定局。專家預報信心暴增的鐵證在於：近幾週以來，大量且極其厚實的巨型熱海暖水團（Warm Water Pool）已在赤道中東太平洋的暗湧深處快速堆積。這波暗濤洶湧的龐大能量終將全部翻湧、傾瀉至海表，為聖嬰現象的爆發持續不間斷地注入毀滅性的升溫燃料。</p>
    <p class="en-text">El Niño is then likely to strengthen through the summer and fall. The chances of it lasting through winter have also increased to 96%, a near lock that it does so. The boost in confidence is due to the vast pool of warm water that’s built up in the depths of the central and eastern equatorial Pacific in recent weeks.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h2 class="bilingual-header">
  <span class="zh-text">⛈️ 氣象風暴圖譜：當升溫鬼牌狠狠蓋在地球桌上</span>
  <span class="en-text">Global Weather Maps: Playing the Warming Wildcard</span>
</h2>

<div style="text-align: center; margin: 24px 0 24px 0;">
  <img src="https://media.cnn.com/api/v1/images/stellar/prod/02-el-nino-global-impacts-051326.png?c=original" alt="El Nino Global Impact Climatology Map" style="border-radius: 12px; max-width: 100%; box-shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.1);" />
  <p style="font-size: 0.85rem; color: var(--vp-c-text-3); margin-top: 6px;">🗺️ 衝擊預警：聖嬰現象在北半球冬季與南半球夏季的全球潛在乾旱、暴雨及高溫地帶分佈圖譜。</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">根據 CPC 所開出的最新強度機率演算，在 11 月至明年 1 月寒冬肆虐期爆發「超級聖嬰」的概率，已從上個月底發布的四分之一（1 in 4），劇烈拉升到了當下的 **三分之一（1 in 3）** 左右。在數位全球頂級計算機演算的極端預測分支中，今年的超級聖嬰規模，甚至極可能直接刷爆人類有儀器氣象監測史以來的「最高峰紀錄」。</p>
  <p class="en-text">Still, the odds of a Super El Niño between November and January have increased from a 1 in 4 chance last month to about a 1 in 3 chance in the latest strength probabilities from the CPC. Some typically reliable computer models show this year’s potential Super El Niño could even be the strongest on record.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">倘若模型一語成讖，這將是自 2015 至 2016 年度之後，近十年來全球迎來的首個「超級聖嬰」。當年那場載入史冊的怪獸氣候是 NOAA 自 1950 年開創系統化檔案庫以來的最高威權強度紀錄，其他歷史上的極端氣候年還包含令人談之色變的 1997-98、1982-83 以及 1972-73 年。值得警覺的是：即便今年最終稍微與「超級」桂冠擦身而過，這依然會被百分之百認定是一次威力非同小可的「強大」聖嬰災害事件。</p>
  <p class="en-text">It would be the first Super El Niño since 2015-2016, which was the strongest in National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration records dating to 1950. Others include 1997-1998, 1982-1983 and 1972-1973. Even if this El Niño falls short of “super” status, it’s still likely to be a strong one.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">聖嬰現象此刻最讓人毛骨悚然的重錘效應即是「火烤全球」：它無疑已在地球大氣物理的賭盤上重重地押下了籌碼，將 2026 年或 2027 年成為人類氣象觀測史上「最炙熱一年」的推算機率直接頂到了峰值。NOAA 甫於本週一坦白指出，今年高機率已預定進榜人類氣溫歷史排行的「前五高」，且上述推估甚至還沒能將聖嬰現象這張最具破壞潛能的發燒「狂怒鬼牌」給徹底核算在內。</p>
  <p class="en-text">A more likely impact is global heat: El Niño is loading the dice toward 2026 or 2027 becoming Earth’s warmest on record. It’s already “very likely” this year will be one of the five-warmest on record NOAA said on Monday, and that’s not yet accounting for El Niño’s warming wild card factor.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">在氣候劇烈重組的全球格局中，無論強烈還是超級，今年聖嬰事件帶來的「颶風季大洗牌」已箭在弦上：在大西洋盆地與加勒比海域，由於高空的剪切風被聖嬰激化，那邊通常會迎來風暴發展的天然殺手屏障，因而導致熱帶颶風量大幅收縮萎縮；但相反的，對於赤道中太平洋與東太平洋的民眾與島國而言，風暴季的演算法極可能被完全逆轉為超常的忙碌與肆虐，夏威夷和北美西南岸勢必得繃緊神經迎戰。</p>
  <p class="en-text">Strong or super, these are the weather conditions El Niño could impact into early next year. Hurricane season flip-flop: Stronger El Niños often produce storm-killing conditions in the Caribbean and tropical Atlantic, resulting in fewer tropical storms and hurricanes there. It’s the opposite story in the central and eastern Pacific Ocean where hurricane season is usually busier.</p>
</div>

<div class="bilingual-paragraph">
  <p class="zh-text">降雨與冷熱的鐘擺同樣將偏盪至光譜的兩極：今年夏季，整個南亞次大陸與東南亞諸國的季風降水恐將雪崩式告急，並與旱魔長驅直入的加勒比海群島遙相呼應。而在遼闊的亞洲中南部及東亞地帶，暖冬和乾燥枯水冬的疊加考驗已是常態性的劇本。與此同時，當 12 月至明年 2 月南半球的夏日熾烈襲來時，非洲東南部的旱象也極有可能被一舉引爆。氣象亂世，已在步步逼近。</p>
  <p class="en-text">Wet, dry and temperature extremes: In summer, monsoon rain is reduced in India and southeast Asia. The Caribbean also often sees growing drought. Warm and dry winters are typical in parts of southern and eastern Asia. Drought conditions could grow in Southeast Africa during the Southern Hemisphere summer from December to February.</p>
</div>
